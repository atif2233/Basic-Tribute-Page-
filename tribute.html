A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man Of India

avul pakir jainulabdeen abdul kalam (15 october 1931 - 27 july 2015) was an indian aerospace scientist whi serverd as a 11th president of india from 2002 to 2007. he was born and raised in rameswaram tamil nadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. he spent the next four decade as a scientist and science administrator. mainly at the defence research and development organisation (DRDO) and indian space researh organisation (ISRO) and was intimately involved in india's civilian space programme and military missile development efforts. [1] he thus came to be known as the missile man of india for his work on the development of bsllistic missile and launch vhicle technology. [2][3][4] he also played pivotal organisational, technical and political role in india's pokhran-II nuclear test in 1998 the first since the original nuclear test by india in 1974.
